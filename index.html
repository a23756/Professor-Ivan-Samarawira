About Me

This book was written for publication in America, and its copyright No. TXU-875-609 was registered in the Copyright Office, Library of Congress, USA in 1998. Its publication was unduly delayed for several reasons. The main reasons at that time were the prevailing religious and bioethical considerations. Additionally, the ban on human cloning prompted the postponement of its publication. Furthermore, my Literary Agent-The Seymour Agency, N.Y. USA did not pursue its publication following my return to Sri Lanka. Now that the ban on stem cell research has been lifted and the basic techniques presented in this book for Nuclear Somatic Transfer (NST) or cloning, have not changed the publication of this book now appears to be timely. The sequencing of the genetic code of the 23 pairs of chromosomes comprising the human genome has been completed by Celera Genomics, Inc. Maryland, USA, and the National Human /genome Research Institute, USA. The clock has turned the full circle and cannot be turned back. The advantages of cloning far outweigh any disadvantages based on ethical, religious or other considerations. This book gives a concise account of such advantages to human medicine. In Vitro Fertilization’ (IVF) fertilization of an egg with sperm in a Petri dish outside the womb of the mother was discovered in 1978 with the birth of the frst IVF baby, Louise Brown. However, religious and bioethical considerations prevented the popularization of the technique for several years. IVF is now accepted as an alternative reproduction technique. Dr. Robert Edwards who discovered IVF in 1978 was awarded the Nobel Prize in Medicine only in 2010, ie 22 years after IVF was discovered although the basic 1978 techniques remains unchanged in 2010, and thousands of children the world over have been created through IVF. In Sri Lanka, a 42 percent success rate has been reported with the birth of 150 children through IVF. The basic techniques used in Embryo Reconstruction or cloning discovered by Professor Ian Wilmut in 1997, and described in this book haven’t changed. Nuclear Somatic Transfer (NST) or the Cloning Method offers promise to infertile couples who might not like Artifcial Insemination Donor (AID) or even invitro-fertilization. This book overviews latest Technologies of Advanced Research by leading World Renowned Scientists who have attempted to demonstrate the enormous powers now available to man to design Life at will. Einstein is reported to have said that “God does not play dice with the Universe.’’ It would seem that God has now empowered man with Advanced Technologies to even direct the future evolution of man.
